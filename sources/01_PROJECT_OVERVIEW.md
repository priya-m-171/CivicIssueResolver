# Civic Issue Resolver (Citizen App) - Project Overview

## 1. Elevator Pitch
"Citizen" is a comprehensive, end-to-end mobile platform designed to bridge the gap between ordinary citizens experiencing civic problems (like potholes, broken streetlights, or waste management issues) and the municipal workforce responsible for fixing them. 

Our goal is simple: **Transparency, Speed, and Accountability.**

## 2. The Problem We Solve
Traditional methods of reporting civic issues are tedious, involve significant paperwork, and lack transparency. Citizens often don't know if their complaint was registered, and municipal authorities struggle to organize and dispatch workers efficiently.

## 3. Our Solution
Citizen app provides a seamless digital workflow:
1. **Identification:** A citizen spots an issue, snaps a photo, and the app's AI automatically categorizes and prioritizes the issue.
2. **Geolocation:** The exact GPS coordinates are attached instantly.
3. **Dispatch:** The system automatically routes the issue to the relevant authority who assigns it to a nearby municipal field worker.
4. **Resolution:** The worker navigates to the location using integrated maps, fixes the problem, and uploads "Proof of Completion" with a live photo and a digital e-signature.
5. **Transparency:** The citizen is notified in real-time that their issue has been resolved.

## 4. Key Takeaways for the Presentation
- **AI-Powered Diagnostics:** We utilize Gemini 2.5 Flash to automatically detect issue categories and severity from citizen photos, minimizing manual data entry.
- **Real-Time Map Integration:** OpenStreetMap integration ensures exact pinpointing of problems, saving hours of worker search time.
- **Cryptographic E-Signatures:** Touching the device screen generates a certified completion signature, completely removing the need for physical paperwork.
- **Cross-Platform:** Built with Flutter, this runs flawlessly on both iOS and Android natively.
